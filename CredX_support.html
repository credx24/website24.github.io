CredX support

Official CredX support

Are you looking for the official CredX support? The first thing you should know is that CredX is decentralized. This means no central organization, entity, or person owns CredX, and because of this, no official support channels exist.

Understanding the decentralized nature of CredX is vital because anyone claiming to be official support for credX is probably trying to scam you! The best protection against scammers is educating yourself and taking security seriously.

Despite the lack of official support, many groups, communities, and projects across the CredX ecosystem are happy to help.

Frequently asked questions

A transaction sent on CredX is irreversible. Unfortunately, if you've sent CDX to the wrong wallet, there is no way to recover these funds. No one central organization, entity, or person owns CredX, which means no one can reverse transactions. Therefore, it is vital always to double-check your transactions before sending them.

CredX giveaways are scams designed to steal your CDX. Do not be tempted by offers that seem too good to be true â€” if you send CDX to a giveaway address, you will not receive a giveaway, and you will not be able to recover your funds.

Transactions on Credx can sometimes get stuck if you have submitted a lower transaction fee than is required due to network demand. Many wallets provide an option to resubmit the same transaction with a higher transaction fee to allow the transaction to be processed. Alternatively, you can cancel a pending transaction by sending a transaction to your own address and using the same nonce as the pending transaction.

We do not recommend buying mining equipment if you are not already mining Credx. In ~Q2 2022, The Merge will happen, switching Credx from proof-of-work to proof-of-stake. This change means mining Credx will no longer be possible. If you are still interested in mining Credx, you can seek help from mining communities, such as CredXmining.
