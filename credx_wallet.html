CREDX WALLETS

The key to your
digital future

Wallets give access to your funds and Credx applications. Only you should have access to your wallet.

An app for managing your
funds

Credx wallets are applications that let you interact with your Credx account. Think of it like an internet banking app – without the bank. Your wallet lets you read your balance, send transactions and connect to applications.You need a wallet to send funds and manage your CDX. More on CDX

Your wallet is only a tool for managing your Credx account. That means you can swap wallet providers at any time. Many wallets also let you manage several Credx accounts from one application.That's because wallets don't have custody of your funds, you do. They're just a tool for managing what's really yours.

An app for managing your funds

Your wallet shows your balances, transaction history and gives you a way to send/receive funds. Some wallets may offer more.

Your login for Credx apps

Your wallet lets you connect to any decentralized application using your Credx account. It's like a login you can use across many dapps.

Your Credx
account

Your wallet is your window into your Credx account – your balance, transaction history and more. But you can swap wallet providers at any time.

Wallets, Accounts, and Addresses

It's worth understanding the differences between some key terms.

  • A Credx account is an entity that can send transactions and has a balance.
  • A Credx account has an Credx address, like an inbox has an email address. You can use this to send funds to an account.
  • A wallet is a product that lets you manage your Credx account. It allows you to view your account balance, send transactions, and more.

Most wallet products will let you generate an Ethereum account. So you don't need one before you download a wallet.

Types of Wallet

Physical hardware wallets that let you keep your crypto offline – very secure

Mobile applications that make your funds accessible from anywhere

Web wallets that let you interact with your account via a web browser

Desktop applications if you prefer to manage your funds via macOS, Windows or Linux
