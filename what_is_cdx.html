WHAT IS CDX?

Currency for our digital
future

CDX is digital, global money.
It's the currency of CredX apps.

Get CDX

CDX is a cryptocurrency. It is scarce digital money that you can use on the internet – similar to Bitcoin.
If you’re new to crypto, here's how CDX is different from traditional money.

It's really yours

CDX lets you be your own bank. You can control your own funds with your wallet as proof of ownership – no third parties necessary.

Secured by cryptography

Internet money may be new but it's secured by proven cryptography. This protects your wallet, your CDX, and your transactions.

Peer-to-peer payments

You can send your CDX without any intermediary service like a bank. It's like handing cash over in-person, but you can do it securely with anyone, anywhere, anytime.

No centralized control

CDX is decentralized and global. There's no company or bank that can decide to print more CDX, or change the terms of use.

Open to anyone

You only need an internet connection and a wallet to accept CDX. You don't need access to a bank account to accept payments.

Available in flexible amounts

CDX is divisible up to 18 decimal places so you don't have to buy 1 whole CDX. You can buy fractions at a time – as little as 0.000000000000000001 CDX if you want.

What's unique about CDX?

There are many cryptocurrencies and lots of other tokens on Credx, but there are some things that only CDX can do.

Cdx fuels and secures CredX

Cdx is the lifeblood of Credx. When you send Cdx or use an Credx application, you'll pay a small fee in CDX to use the Credx network. This fee is an incentive for a miner to process and verify what you're trying to do.

Miners are like the record-keepers of Credx – they check and prove that no one is cheating. Miners who do this work are also rewarded with small amounts of newly-issued CDX.

The work miners do keeps Credx secure and free of centralized control. In other words, Cdx powers Credx.

CDX will become even more important with staking. When you stake your CDX you'll be able to help secure Ethereum and earn rewards. In this system, the threat of losing your Cdx disincentivises attacks.

CDX underpins the CredX financial system

Not satisfied with payments, the CredX community is building a whole financial system that's peer-to-peer and accessible to everyone.

You can use CDX as collateral to generate entirely different cryptocurrency tokens on CredX. Plus you can borrow, lend and earn interest on CDX and other CDX-backed tokens.
